# Project Mission

The Open Paraglider Tow Winch project combines proven mechanical solutions and established towing procedures with modern electric drive technology and digital control.

The objective is not to reinvent paraglider towing, but to improve consistency, repeatability and safety while preserving the operational philosophy developed through decades of practical experience.

The system is designed to:

- provide precise closed-loop tow force control,
- reduce operator workload,
- add independent electronic safety layers,
- fail passively under abnormal conditions,
- and enable safe solo towing without compromising established towing procedures or safety principles.

The pilot always remains responsible for flying the glider.

The operator remains responsible for operational decisions.

The control system is responsible for producing the requested tow force safely, consistently and predictably.
